Fleet Onboarding Options Review

Workshop to optimize the onboarding workflow for fleet partners (leasing companies, fuel wholesalers, fleet aggregators), as well as corporate customers (B2B/B2B2C/B2B2E).
Goal: fast setup manageable ops reliable for drivers
🎯 Guiding Goal

We want an onboarding flow that is fast for the fleet partner to set up, manageable for our internal teams to operate, and reliable for drivers at the point of charging — with minimal escalations once live.

As you respond, consider three perspectives:

🏢
Your Department
What works and what creates friction from your team's point of view
🤝
Corporate Fleet Partner
What they need for a smooth, confident setup experience
🚗
Individual Driver
What ensures a seamless, frustration-free charging experience on the ground
🤝 Our Goal Today

Build cross-departmental alignment. We want everyone to understand why Sales offers different onboarding options to meet varied customer needs — and we want support functions to surface the operational challenges they currently face or anticipate.

Your Role

Review the onboarding options and share your insights on the best approach for fleet user onboarding and their ongoing charging experience on our EV charging platform.
